Meet Our Founders

Satendra Chaurasiya

Co-Founder & CEO

Satendra is a visionary leader passionate about revolutionizing observability solutions. With deep expertise in software development and distributed systems, he founded CloudNova to democratize enterprise-grade monitoring tools. His mission is to make observability 100x more efficient, helping companies reduce costs by 90% while maintaining world-class performance.

Sanket Nawale

Co-Founder & CTO

Sanket is a technical architect and open-source advocate with expertise in building high-performance infrastructure. As the CTO of CloudNova, he leads the development of cutting-edge log analytics and observability technology. He is also the founder of 8-Bit Cat, where he applies his strong proficiency in FastAPI and cloud automation to create scalable, reliable systems. His work in satellite communication systems and MATLAB simulations brings a unique perspective to solving complex distributed system challenges at scale.
